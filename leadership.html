Leadership & Strategic Partners

Leadership at Basileus Satores is senior-led and deeply involved in every transformation program. We do not delegate strategic stewardship. We lead it — directly, quietly, and with accountability.

Sam Mbowa

Senior Managing Partner — Advisory, Revenue & Growth

Sam Mbowa is a global sales and strategy executive with over 15 years of leadership across pharmaceuticals, medical diagnostics, and strategic transformation spanning Africa, the U.S., and Europe. His work has consistently focused on strengthening commercial ecosystems, redesigning revenue engines, and aligning partners across public and private mandates to create measurable value.

Sam has led large-scale, multimillion-dollar engagements — including a $60M public–private health transformation partnership involving government ministries, global funders, NGO consortiums, and multinational suppliers. His ability to navigate complex stakeholder landscapes and reconcile competing priorities has enabled organizations to unlock efficiency, sustainability, and long-term viability.

His leadership philosophy is anchored in disciplined execution, operational clarity, and deliberate alignment — ensuring that strategy does not merely “guide,” but produces concrete outcomes.

Allan Otieno

Senior Managing Partner — Organizational Architecture & Strategic Insights

Allan Otieno began his career in Kenya’s public health research systems — grounding his approach in data integrity, field evidence, and strategic analysis. This foundation evolved into a global leadership journey spanning East Africa, the Middle East, Europe, North Africa, Asia, Latin America, Brazil, Ukraine, and Russia — guiding commercial effectiveness and organizational restructuring at scale.

Allan’s work integrates intelligence reporting, culture design, decision architecture, and operating model refinement. He has built market intelligence frameworks, redesigned commercial teams across regions, architected CRM and digital engagement systems, and led restructuring initiatives across multinational organizations — always with a quiet clarity and exacting precision.

He specializes in embedding high-performance culture, leadership maturity, and institutional foresight — ensuring organizations not only excel in the present, but are structurally prepared for the next horizon of growth.
